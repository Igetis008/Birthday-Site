Happy Birthday, Rishika!

May your pages be filled with wonder and your horizons be limitless.

To the one who reads the stars like text,
And wonders where the path leads next,
With maps tucked neatly in her mind,
And warmth that leaves the dark behind.

May wild winds guide your flying plane,
Through sunset gold and summer rain,
A cute, sweet light in a world so wide,
I'm glad to travel by your side.

Another year, a clean white page,
A beautiful, whimsical, golden stage.
Write it well, my lovely guide,
With love forever close beside.

✈️

With all my love and wishes,

Aric
